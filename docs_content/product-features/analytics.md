Highlight provides analytics for your app to give you an idea of who and what are users doing. We provide the following:

*   Sessions per Day

*   Errors per Day

*   Referrers

*   Top Users

*   New Users

*   Device Type

*   Browser Type

*   Session Durations

*   Operating System
